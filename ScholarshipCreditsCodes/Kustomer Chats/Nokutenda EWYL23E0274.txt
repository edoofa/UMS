Even after the taunts and humiliation,Zara didn't give up on her dreams ,she kept trying.One fruitful day she helped Mr Alex,an old crippled man who had been isolated by the villagers,there began her journey to success.Mr Alex was a graduate but due to his condition he was rejected everywhere he went in search of internship till he became old,everyone in the village knew Zara's story including him,he knew.He offered to help her throughout her studies only if she could build self confidence inorder to combat limiting beliefs.Zara was a smart student but what she lacked was confidence, determination and dedication which Mr Alex had to implant in her.He kept giving her assignments to do and then she had to learn on time management and planning as she would be punished if she doesn't complete her assignments accurately.Zara had to work hard because this was her last chance,so she was told by her parents,she became committed and dedicated to her school work that when she wrote her final exams for the sixth time she knew it in her heart that this was her new start to a successful journey.Results came and she had passed with flying colours.She became the role model to every student at Blantae senior school that till date her story is used as an example.

She became a true source of inspiration to everyone as they traced back to all her life encounters.She had to come back to see the impact her life had instilled in other students and each time she looked back a wave of satisfaction was always a mark on her face.She then understood the sayings "failure is an opportunity to start again but this time more intelligently making success a state of mind" and "a true soldier is noticed by a million bruises"Her life was as difficult but she pulled through because of consistency.
